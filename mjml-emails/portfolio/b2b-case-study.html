The Problem: 12 Hours a Week Wasted Vetting Leads
Before implementing FlowSync, the sales development team at Apex Legal was drowning in manual admin. Every time a user downloaded their "2026 Compliance Checklist", a sales rep had to manually research company size, check LinkedIn for job titles, and copy-paste an email outreach.
The result? Average speed-to-lead hovered around 18 hours. In modern B2B SaaS, a lead is 10x less likely to respond after just 5 minutes of wait time. High-intent buyers were going cold and signing up for competitor demos instead.
