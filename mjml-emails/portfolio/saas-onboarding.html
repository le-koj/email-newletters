Tired of endless notification pings? Let's explore the async communication framework that helps high-performing teams protect deep focus.
 
The Cost of Context Switching
Every time a teammate interrupts you with a Slack DM or an ad-hoc Zoom invite, it takes an average of 23 minutes to regain deep focus. When this happens all day, team productivity doesn't just dip—it tanks.
Async-first companies don't communicate less; they communicate differently. They batch notifications and document decisions in a centralized system. Here is the exact framework we use at TaskHive to keep teams aligned without the friction of real-time distractions.

3 Hours Limit
• Async SLA: Respond to internal project comments within 3 hours.
• Urgent flags: Only use direct notifications for blocking issues.
• No guilt: Snooze notifications during designated deep focus.
